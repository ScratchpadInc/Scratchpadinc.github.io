Frequently asked questions

How long do searches take?

Most searches return in 2-10 seconds. Bloom filters let us skip 95-99% of partitions, so we only fetch data that's likely to match. No restore delays—Glacier Instant Retrieval has millisecond first-byte latency.

What if I need real-time alerting?

Keep your existing stack for that. Glaciersearch is for long-term retention and investigations—not real-time monitoring. Send logs to both: Datadog for alerts, Glaciersearch for the archive.

How does data stay in my account?

We deploy Lambda functions and S3 buckets in your AWS account via CloudFormation. Your logs never leave your VPC.

What about compliance certifications?

Everything runs in your AWS account, so you inherit your existing compliance posture—HIPAA, SOC 2, PCI-DSS, FedRAMP, whatever you've already configured.

Isn't Glacier slow?

You're thinking of Glacier Deep Archive (12-hour restores). We use S3 Glacier Instant Retrieval for millisecond access. Our efficient search index keeps retrieval fees low.

What log formats do you support?

JSON, newline-delimited JSON, and common structured formats. We auto-detect schemas and optimize compression per field type. Custom formats can be supported—reach out.
